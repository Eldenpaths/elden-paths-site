The Law of Elden Paths

Fog of Discovery™

The world begins hidden to all. Only those who walk the land reveal its form. Each player's view is unique and persistent.

Echo System™

Every step, wound, and word becomes a memory in the world. These echoes can be seen by others if they retrace your steps.

Oath-Binding™

Special classes may bind locations through ritual or conquest. These become protected, named zones until challenged or undone.
