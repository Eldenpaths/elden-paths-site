Elden Paths™ Quick Player Guide

Welcome to the beta build of Elden Paths™, a persistent roleplaying world shaped by your actions.

Getting Started

  • Type type to view character classes
  • Use photo to upload your appearance
  • Upload a pet if you want a real companion
  • Roll or assign your stats
  • When ready, type begin

Game Basics

  • The world is shared and persistent
  • Your actions affect lore and other players
  • Fog of Discovery™ hides unexplored areas
  • Every sketch, building, and wound is permanent

Need Help?

Check for updates, guides, and map tools at eldenpaths.com

This game is in beta. Expect some wild stuff. Enjoy the journey.
